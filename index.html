ABOUT ME

I’m Marika. I'm a punk, nonbinary tattoo artist.
Fueled by cyberpunk dreams and obsessed with gym.
I travel, I ink, I share my soul in every piece.
Since I first picked up a coil machine at eighteen,
I’ve never looked back.
What started as a spark of curiosity
quickly grew into a lifelong passion and career.
Over the years, I built and ran my own tattoo studio,
honing my craft and creating meaningful art
for clients who trusted me to tell their stories on skin.
Now, I find myself at a new crossroads—eager to pack my bags,
hit the road, and immerse myself in fresh experiences.
I’m driven by a hunger to explore underground, connect with vibrant cultures,
and push my artistry in unexpected directions.
The world is my next canvas, and I can’t wait to see where this journey takes me.
