ABOUT ME

I’m Marika. I'm a punk, nonbinary tattoo artist.
Fueled by cyberpunk dreams and obsessed with gym.
I travel, I ink, I share my soul in every piece.
Since I first picked up a coil machine at eighteen, I’ve never looked back.

Over the years, I built and ran my own tattoo studio, honing my craft and creating meaningful art.

Now I’m eager to pack my bags, hit the road, and immerse myself in fresh experiences.
